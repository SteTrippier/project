User Information and GDPR

This page outlines the information stored about users of our system and how it is used in accordance with UK GDPR.

User Information

Employees:

We collect the following information about employees:

  • Username: Used for identification and authentication purposes.
  • First Name: Used to address you personally within the system.
  • Last Name: Used to identify you uniquely and for display purposes.
  • Email: Used for communication and account recovery purposes.
  • Telephone: Used for work-related communication.
  • Address: Used to contact you and assign tasks.
  • Department: Helps in managing work assignments.
  • Role: Determines your responsibilities and access rights.
  • Working Days: Specifies your work schedule.
  • Status: Indicates your current employment status.
  • Remaining Annual Leave: Tracks your available leave days.

System Users:

We collect the following information about system users:

  • Username: Used for identification and authentication purposes.
  • Password Hash: Stored securely to ensure account security.
  • Role: Determines access rights within the system.

Usage of Information

The collected information is used as follows:

  • Username: Your username is used to uniquely identify you and grant you access to the system.
  • Password Hash: We store password hashes to ensure the security of your account. Your actual password is never stored in plain text.

UK GDPR Compliance

We are committed to protecting your data and privacy in accordance with UK GDPR:

  • We collect and store only the necessary information required for system functionality.
  • Your data is stored securely and is accessible only to authorized personnel.
  • We do not share your data with third parties unless required by law.
  • You have the right to access, update, or delete your personal data

If you have any questions or concerns regarding your data, please contact our Steven Trippier.
